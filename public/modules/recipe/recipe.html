Spaghetti Bolognese

  • Serving size: 2
  • Difficulty: Easy
  • Time: 35 min

Craving Italian? Try this authentic and delicious spaghetti bolognese.

- Chef Chris

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 medium onion, diced
  • 2 garlic cloves, peeled and coarsely chopped
  • 1 celery stalk, chopped
  • 1 carrot, chopped
  • 1 lb ground chuck beef
  • 1 (28-ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup flat-leaf Italian parsely
  • 8 fresh basil leaves, chiffonade
  • 1/4 cup freshly grated pecorino romano or parmesan cheese

Steps

  • 1. Add extra-virgin olive oil to a 6 quart pot until almost smoking.
  • 2. Add onions and garlic, and saute over medium heat until the onions become very soft, about 8 minutes.
  • 3. Add celery and carrots, and saute for 5 minutes.
  • 4. Raise heat to high and add the ground beef. Season with salt and pepper. Saute, stirring frequently and breaking up any large lumps. Cook until meat is no longer pink.
  • 5. Add tomatoes, parsley and basil, and cook over medium low heat until the sauce thickens.
  • 6. Finish sauce with pecorino romano or parmean cheese. Check for seasoning.
